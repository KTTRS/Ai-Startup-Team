## Risk Factors and How We Answer Them

**"The AI behavioral health space is crowded."**
We are not competing with behavioral health apps. We are competing with nothing — because no one is providing relational continuity infrastructure to Medicaid populations at scale. Our closest "competitors" are condition-specific apps with 20% DAU. We have 84%.

**"How do you protect against a health system building this in-house?"**
The same way Salesforce protects against companies building their own CRM. The longitudinal data we generate is the moat. You can build the interface. You cannot replicate 3 years of Signal Score data for 500,000 members.

**"What if MCOs don't pay $25-30 PMPM?"**
We've already had conversations where $25 PMPM was below the number they expected to pay. This isn't aspirational pricing — it's conservative pricing for a category that has no existing benchmark.

**"The regulatory environment for AI in healthcare is uncertain."**
Yes. And we built our ethics framework before anyone asked us to. We don't use clinical diagnostic language. We are a relational tool, not a clinical one. That distinction is intentional and defensible.

**"Can a small team execute this?"**
The team that built what we've built — 84% DAU, NRI™ framework, 10 interaction modes, Signal Score — is not a small team by output. It's a small team by headcount. The seed round solves the headcount. The output demonstrates we know how to work.
