## Q2 2026 — The Quarter That Decides Everything (Apr 1 – Jun 30)

Q2 is not a planning exercise. Q2 is the quarter that determines whether SAINTE survives to build what it's supposed to build. We need: a funded seed round, a signed enterprise contract, and real user data that proves the product works. Everything else is noise.

### Objective 1: Prove Siani works with measurable outcomes

**Why this matters:** No MCO will sign a contract without outcomes data. No investor will write a seed check without proof this isn't just a chatbot. We have the traction numbers — we need Signal Score data to tell the story.

**Key Results:**
- KR1.1: 500+ users complete at least 10 sessions in the beta cohort
- KR1.2: Average DAU maintained at or above 80% across the beta cohort
- KR1.3: Signal Score data shows measurable directional improvement in at least 2 of 5 dimensions (T/A/R/C/S) over 30-day period — documented and statistically framed
- KR1.4: 3 user case studies documented (with consent) showing before/after relational continuity patterns
- KR1.5: ER visit proxy metric (self-reported crisis escalation rate) baseline established for pilot population

**Why this matters:** One signed LOI or paid pilot changes everything — our fundraise, our narrative, our credibility. We have the traction. We need to convert it into a contract.

**Key Results:**

- KR2.2: At least 1 paid pilot contract executed (even if small — $10K–$50K) or $25 PMPM rate validated in writing
