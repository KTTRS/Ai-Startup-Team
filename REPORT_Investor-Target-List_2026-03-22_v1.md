## How to Read This List

Three tiers:
- **Tier 1:** Lead candidates. Check size $250K–$750K. These investors set terms and signal credibility to everyone else. We work hardest here.
- **Tier 2:** Strong follow-on. Check size $100K–$250K. These fill the round and often have strategic value beyond capital.
- **Tier 3:** Mission-aligned small checks. $10K–$100K. They believe in the mission, they have network value, and they help us hit the round ceiling.

Warm path column uses: WARM (have a real connection), LUKEWARM (one degree, needs activation), COLD (no connection, need to earn it).

**The rule:** We lead with traction, not story. Every first outreach includes at least one of: 84% DAU, 14% ER reduction, $25-30 PMPM enterprise model. Lead with proof, then let the story land.
